Transmission (Death Claim) of Shares

At DeepamFinvest, we understand the importance of streamlining the process of transferring shares or bonds to surviving family members, a procedure commonly referred to as ' Transmission.' This process involves the following key steps:

Document Submission

The initial step typically entails providing essential documents such as the Death Certificate of the deceased shareholder. Additionally, depending on the market value of the shares or bonds, other documents may be required, including a Legal Heir Certificate, a Will, or a Succession Certificate. These documents serve to establish the legal entitlement of the surviving family member.

NOC Requirement

In some cases, a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from surviving heirs who are not the primary claimant may be necessary. This NOC ensures that all legal heirs are in agreement with the transmission process.

Our dedicated team at DeepamFinvest is here to facilitate and simplify the entire transmission process, ensuring that the necessary paperwork is in order and that the transition is carried out smoothly and efficiently. We aim to provide you with expert guidance and support to navigate this often-complex procedure with ease.

Claim of Shares & Dividend from IEPF

We are well-versed in the standard procedure for reclaiming dividends and shares that have been transferred to the Investor Education and Protection Fund (IEPF) due to non-claim for seven consecutive years. This process involves the following key aspects:

Transfer to IEPF

When dividends remain unclaimed for a continuous period of seven years, both the unclaimed dividends and the ownership of the shares associated with those dividends are legally transferred to the IEPF.

Reclaim Procedure

Reclaiming these dividends and the underlying shares requires following a standard procedure outlined by the IEPF. This procedure typically includes submitting an application for the refund of dividends and the transfer of shares.

Our team at DeepamFinvest is here to guide you through the process step by step, ensuring that all necessary documents and requirements are met. We aim to make the reclamation process as smooth and hassle-free as possible, allowing you to regain your dividends and share ownership without unnecessary complications.
